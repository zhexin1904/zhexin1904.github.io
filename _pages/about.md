I am currently a master's student in the Northeastern University Robotics program, and I am also a graduate student in the Northeastern University Robust Autonomy Lab[NEURAL](https://neural.lab.northeastern.edu/), under the direction of.[Professor David M. Rosen](https://david-m-rosen.github.io/)

I am broadly interested in topics related to state estimation for mobile robotics systems. Currently, I am focusing on Lidar-inertial navigation, multi-sensor fusion for localization and mapping.
